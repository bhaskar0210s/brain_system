## Operating Principles

- L1 changes are rare and require formal evaluation.
- L2 is where most structured understanding lives and evolves.
- L4 (importance scoring + relationships) is the primary mechanism for smart retrieval and easy reorganization.
- The LLM has full authority to extend L2 structure during consolidation.
